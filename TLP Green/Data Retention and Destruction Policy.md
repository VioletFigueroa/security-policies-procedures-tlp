# Policy Title {#policy-title}

Data Retention and Destruction Policy

# TLP Classification {#tlp-classification}

TLP: Green

# Purpose {#purpose}

This policy establishes guidelines for retaining data only as long as necessary while ensuring secure destruction when no longer needed to protect privacy and comply with PIPEDA.

# Scope {#scope}

This policy applies to all data collected or stored by BC Ferries.

# Policy Statement {#policy-statement}

* **Retention Periods**:
  * Customer PII retained for five years; financial records retained for seven years.
* **Secure Storage**:
  * Data encrypted at rest; access restricted based on roles.
* **Destruction Methods**:
  * Digital records overwritten; physical storage devices degaussed or shredded.

# Responsibilities {#responsibilities}

* **Data Owners**: Define retention requirements.
* **IT Department**: Implement secure storage/destruction methods.

# Compliance {#compliance}

Non-compliance may result in regulatory fines under PIPEDA or reputational damage.

# Review and Approval {#review-and-approval}

This policy will be reviewed annually by the IT Security Team. Updates must be approved by the CISO.
