# Policy Title {#policy-title}

Network Segmentation Policy

# TLP Classification {#tlp-classification}

TLP: Green

# Purpose {#purpose}

This policy minimizes the risk of ransomware spreading across BC Ferries’ network by isolating critical OT systems from IT systems.

# Scope {#scope}

This policy applies to all IT/OT systems within BC Ferries’ infrastructure.

# Policy Statement {#policy-statement}

* Segmentation Implementation:
  * IT/OT networks are separated using firewalls or VLANs (Transport Canada, 2021).
* Traffic Monitoring:
  * Intrusion Detection Systems (IDS) monitor traffic between segments (National Institute of Standards and Technology \[NIST\], 2012).
* Access Restrictions:
  * Remote access to OT systems is prohibited unless pre-approved through secure channels.
* Audits:
  * Semi-annual audits ensure compliance with segmentation requirements.

# Responsibilities {#responsibilities}

* **IT Department**: Design and maintain segmentation configurations; monitor traffic.
* **Operations Team**: Communicate operational requirements to IT.

# Compliance {#compliance}

Non-compliance may result in disciplinary actions or regulatory penalties under Transport Canada’s DFSR.
