# Policy Title {#policy-title}

Backup Policy

# TLP Classification {#tlp-classification}

TLP: Green

# Purpose {#purpose}

This policy ensures that BC Ferriesâ€™ critical systems and data are regularly backed up to enable recovery in case of ransomware attacks, system failures, or disasters.

# Scope {#scope}

This policy applies to:

* All critical systems, including ticketing platforms, OT systems, and customer databases.
* Onsite and offsite backup solutions.

# Policy Statement {#policy-statement}

* Backup Frequency:
  * Nightly backups for critical systems; full backups weekly.
* Storage Locations:
  * Backups stored onsite and offsite; at least one offline copy required.
* Testing:
  * Quarterly restoration tests ensure data integrity.
* Access Control:
  * Backup systems restricted to authorized personnel with MFA enabled.
* Retention Periods:
  * Daily backups retained for seven days; weekly backups retained for four weeks; monthly backups retained for one year (Cybersecurity and Infrastructure Security Agency \[CISA\], 2021)..

# Responsibilities {#responsibilities}

* IT Department: Manage backup processes and conduct tests.
* Data Owners: Identify critical systems requiring backups.

# Compliance {#compliance}

Non-compliance may lead to disciplinary actions or regulatory fines under PIPEDA (Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada \[PIPEDA\], 2000).

# Review and Approval {#review-and-approval}

This policy will be reviewed annually by the IT Security Team. Updates must be approved by the CISO.
