# Policy Title {#policy-title}

Log Retention Policy

# TLP Classification {#tlp-classification}

TLP: Green

# Purpose {#purpose}

This policy ensures that logs required for incident analysis or regulatory reporting are securely retained for defined periods while protecting sensitive information.

# Scope {#scope}

This policy applies to all logs generated by BC Ferriesâ€™ IT infrastructure.

# Policy Statement {#policy-statement}

* Retention Periods:
  * Security-related logs retained for one year; general logs retained for six months.
* Storage Requirements:
  * Logs stored in a centralized logging solution with encryption at rest.
* Monitoring Logs:
  * Automated tools (e.g., SIEM) review logs daily for anomalies.

# Responsibilities {#responsibilities}

* IT Department manages log storage solutions and ensures compliance with retention policies.
* Security Analysts review logs regularly for suspicious activity.

# Compliance {#compliance}

Non-compliance may result in regulatory fines under PIPEDA or reputational damage.

# Review and Approval {#review-and-approval}

This policy will be reviewed annually by the IT Security Team. Updates must be approved by the CISO.
